ChordPro Syntax Guide
Format 1 — ChordPro (recommended)

Put chords in [brackets] inline before the syllable they land on.

{title: Wish You Were Here} {artist: Pink Floyd} {key: G} {start_of_verse} So, so you [G]think you can [Em]tell, [G]heaven from [Em]hell? {end_of_verse} {start_of_chorus} [C]How I [G]wish you were [D]here {end_of_chorus}
Format 2 — Tab/UG style (auto-converts)

Chord names on a separate line above the lyrics, aligned by position.

[Verse 1] C G Look at the stars Am F Look how they shine for you [Chorus] C G And it was all yellow

{title: Song Title} {artist: Artist Name} {key: G} {tempo: 120} {start_of_verse} {end_of_verse} {start_of_chorus} {end_of_chorus} {comment: A note here} {start_of_tab: Intro} {end_of_tab}
